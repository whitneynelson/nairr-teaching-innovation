## What you'll build today

### Publish your course site

Before turning anything on, do a pre-publish leak check on `course-site`: confirm no answer keys, grading detail, student data, or private prep notes made it in, and re-check your program's Three Hard Rules with your facilitator. Then: **Settings → Pages** → set source branch (usually `main`) → save. Click through every page once it's live — syllabus, schedule, modules, resources, office hours — and confirm nothing 404s and nothing private is visible.

### Reuse this template

Confirm both `course-site` and `course-toolkit` are set as GitHub template repos (**Settings** → check **"Template repository"**). For your next course or cohort: your repo structure, rubric templates, and this Builder Guide carry over cleanly; your learning outcomes, curriculum map, module content, and exam questions need rebuilding each time.

### 90-day implementation plan

Note which toolkit artifacts are still pending, and what department or institutional buy-in you still need.
