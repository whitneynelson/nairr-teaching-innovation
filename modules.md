# What You'll Build

Ten modules, walked through step by step across the week — each one produces a real piece of your course, not a worksheet.

| Module | Title | When | What you'll build |
|---|---|---|---|
| 00–01 | Set Up Your Repos & Environment | Day 1 morning | Your public and private course repos, created and connected to Claude |
| 02 | Learning Outcomes → Curriculum Map | Day 1 afternoon + Day 2 morning | A learning outcomes map and a full curriculum sequence |
| 03 | Draft Your Syllabus | Day 2 midday | A syllabus draft built from your outcomes and schedule |
| 04 | Module Builder | Day 2–4 (three parts) | One full course module — lecture outline, slides, quiz, and assignment material — built end to end as a repeatable pattern |
| 05 | Grading Scale & Rubrics | Day 4 morning | Your grading scale, an assignment rubric template, and an AI-use rubric |
| 06 | Exam Scaffold | Day 4 morning | A midterm exam scaffold with answer key, including HPC-literacy questions |
| 07 | AI for Grading & Attendance | Optional, self-paced | Feedback prompt templates and an attendance pattern template, built on de-identified data |
| 08 | Office Hours Setup | Day 4 afternoon | A booking link and office hours policy, plus LMS export of your materials |
| 09 | Publish Your Course Site | Day 5 morning | Your course site, live on GitHub Pages |
| 10 | Reuse This Template | Day 5 morning | A plan for reusing everything you built for your next course or cohort |

By Friday afternoon, your course site is public, your instructor toolkit is private and complete, and you have a 90-day plan for what comes next.

See the [Agenda](agenda.md) for exact times each day.
