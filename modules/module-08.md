# Module 08: Office Hours Setup

*Feeds: Day 5, 10:15–11:00 AM (shared block with Modules 09 and 10)*

If you already set up Calendly before the event, don't spend the whole 30 minutes here; jump to the Canva/Blackboard export part.

## Step 1: Set up your booking link

Calendly (if created pre-event) or Teams booking, per your institution's setup.

## Step 2: Write your office hours policy

A short paragraph: format (in-person/virtual), what to bring, response-time expectations.

## Step 3: Build `course-site/office-hours.md`

Booking link + policy, published.

## Step 4: Export to Canva & Blackboard

This is where slide decks and other visual materials built in [Module 04](module-04.html) get exported for your LMS.
- If Canva–Blackboard LTI is enabled at your campus, walk through the export now.
- If not, note this as a pending item for your 90-day plan.

## Checkpoint

<ul class="checklist">
<li><code>course-site/office-hours.md</code> has a working booking link and policy</li>
<li>Canva/Blackboard export tested, or flagged as pending institutional setup</li>
</ul>
